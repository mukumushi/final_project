about me

I'm Trish, a UIUX Designer based out of the Washington, D.C. area. I do more than make stuff pretty. I'm a problem solver and an analytical thinker. I'm an advocate for seamless and intuitive user experiences. I like to create end-to-end experiences—from conception (meeting with users, clients, and developers) to wireframes and prototypes, and finally to polished, visually stunning products (with lots of iteration and testing along the way).

Throughout my career, I've had the priviledge of working on very impactful projects, with sometimes very vulnerable users. Whether it's teens that are addicted to smoking, people living with HIV, or military operations fighting critical missions, working on projects that make a difference as been incredibly fulfilling.
